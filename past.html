Background

My Background & Interests

From a young age, I was an energetic student who was passionate about learning. I was exposed to English early because I attended an English kindergarten and later studied English in the United States, which led me to dream of studying abroad.

When I was younger, I was especially interested in arts and sports. I was a swimmer and won many competitions, and I also enjoyed playing the piano because of my mother's influence.

As I grew older, however, I started to think more realistically about my future career. After learning Python in high school and being influenced by my father, I chose to study Artificial Intelligence.
