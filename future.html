What is a quantitative trader

A Quantitative Trader is essentially a "financial engineer" who combines math and data through programming master the market

Why I became interested in this dream

I am interested in becoming a quantitative trader because I have been investing in stocks since I was 14 years old. This experience made me interested in numbers, market patterns, and data-based decision-making. I have always enjoyed mathematics and problem-solving, which is one of the main reasons I am attracted to this career. I also find it exciting that quantitative trading requires quick and logical thinking. My current major in Artificial Intelligence is closely connected to this goal, because it helps me build skills in programming, data analysis, and analytical thinking. I believe this career suits both my interests and strengths.

Skills I want to Develop

  • Financial market knowledge
  • Data analysis
  • Python programming
  • Statistics and mathematics
  • Problem-solving skills
My long term Goals and Plans

My long-term goal is to become a quantitative trader after graduation. Before reaching that goal, I would like to gain practical experience through internships in finance, trading, or data-related roles. I am especially interested in working in a trading company or a financial firm where I can learn more about markets, risk, and data-driven decision-making. I also want to continue improving my skills in mathematics, Python, and data analysis so that I can prepare myself for this career. In the long term, I hope to work in a professional trading environment where I can apply both my technical knowledge and analytical thinking. Through these experiences, I want to grow into a skilled quantitative trader who can make logical and effective decisions in fast-moving markets.

Short-term Goals

My short-term goals are to improve my programming, mathematics, and data analysis skills while I continue my studies at UTS. I also want to learn more about financial markets and how data is used in trading decisions. In the near future, I hope to gain practical experience through internships in finance, trading, or analytics-related roles. These short-term goals will help me build a strong foundation for my long-term goal of becoming a quantitative trader.
